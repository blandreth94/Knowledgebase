Equipment needed: Microphone charger cradle
1. Retrieve the charger cradle from the second drawer (from the top) of the [[FRC/FRC-AV/index#Equipment Locations and Storage Descriptions|Production Drawers]]. 
![[1000014488.webp]]
2. Place the cradle on one of the table surfaces, and plug it into the power strip connected in stage [[FRC/FRC-AV/Track 1/1a. Initial Streaming Unit Setup and Core Cabling|1a]]. 
3. Place unused microphones in the charger. Charge all microphones at least once a day, either during lunch or overnight. 
Stage 2d is complete. [[FRC/FRC-AV/Track 2/2e. Connect Speakers|Proceed to stage 2e]].
