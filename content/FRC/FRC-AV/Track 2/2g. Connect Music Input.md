Supplies needed: AV laptop, headphone cable (1/8" or 3.5mm).

1. Retrieve the AV laptop from the Audio [[FRC/FRC-AV/index#Equipment Locations and Storage Descriptions|AV tote]].
   ![[FRC/FRC-AV/Track 2/Media/1000014504.webp]]
2. Retrieve a headphone cable from the top drawer of the Production Drawers. The cable may also be packed in the bag with the laptop.
3. Connect the headphone output of the laptop to the "3.5mm Audio In" jack on the Streaming Unit.
![[PXL_20251117_193705130_7.webp]]
4. Launch music playback program. If it prompts for a login, please consult with the Equipment Manager for the account credentials.

Stage 2g is complete. [[FRC/FRC-AV/Track 2/2h. Sound Check and Gain Leveling|Proceed to stage 2h]].
